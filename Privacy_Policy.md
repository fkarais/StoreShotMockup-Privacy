Privacy Policy for Play Console Tester Tracker
Last Updated: April 26, 2026

At Play Console Tester Tracker, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Collect
We collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

User Email: Accessed via chrome.identity to identify your account and manage your premium subscription/trial status.
Package Names: When you navigate to a Google Play Console application page, we detect the package name (e.g., com.example.app) to track its testing progress.
Tester Activity: We store the last seen timestamps of your testers in your own dedicated Firestore database to help you track the 14-day testing marathon.
2. How We Use Your Information
To manage your 3-day free trial and premium license.
To provide a real-time dashboard of your testers' activity.
To send you desktop notifications as reminders for your testing streaks.
3. Data Storage (Firebase)
We use Google Firebase (Firestore) to store your app tracking data. Your data is stored securely and is only used to populate your extension dashboard.

4. Third-Party Services
Google Cloud/Identity: Used for authentication.
Gumroad: Used for processing premium payments. We do not store your credit card or billing information.
5. Data Security
We implement security measures to protect your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights
You can request to delete your data at any time by contacting us. Since your settings are stored in chrome.storage.sync, clearing your extension data or uninstalling the extension will remove local settings.

7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support channels provided in our store listing.
